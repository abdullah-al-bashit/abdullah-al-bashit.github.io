\section{Research Interests}
\begin{sectioncontent}
% Statistical Signal Processing, Machine Learning, Inverse Problems , Computational Biology.

I am broadly interested in developing statistical signal processing and machine learning methods to integrate diverse medical data, uncover the biology of complex diseases, and translate those insights into quantitative measures of disease risk and treatment response.

% My research centers on devising novel statistical signal processing and machine learning algorithms, and on adapting established AI/ML approaches for scientific discovery. I developed new methods for analyzing scanning X-ray microdiffraction data collected at NSLS-II, Beamline 16 in collaboration with Lin Yang, with an emphasis on extracting mechanistic insight and producing robust quantitative results.
